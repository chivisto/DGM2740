Welcome to Thai Basil

Thai Basil is a fine Asian cuisine restaurant.

We pride ourselves in our delicious food we have to offer. We sell delicious food and drinks some of these things range from Orange Chicken, Pho, and even finer things like Crab. We also offer vegetarian options if you need. Let us know of any allergy restrictions before you order to insure a safe and comfortable meal.

We do offer catering, dine-in, and take-out so feel free to choose any option! You can also order online through the order tab in the navigation. Feel free to give us a call at 123-555-6789 if you have any questions for us.
